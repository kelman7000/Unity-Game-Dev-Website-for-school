About

Welcome! If you are here that probably means that you want to learn how to make games? Well if that is the case you've come to the right place. Here you are going to learn the essential basics of how to make games in the Unity Engine. You might be asking yourself What are the basics? What exactly will I be learning? How will I be learning? These are all exelent questions, we will provide you with a well structured, linear path to progress down but following this path is up to you. First, you'll learn about the programming laguage that is used to make games in Unity, then come the basics of the Engine itself (how to navigate around and a breef intoroduction into everything else that will be used), after that will be an indepth section going over Animating (How you can create your own game assets), working with sounds (what you can use to make your own music), and you'll even know how to make mobile games after all this!

How it works

After getting introduced to everything we are going to combine all the new skill you've accquired into makeing two project games. One will be a 2D side scroller, and the other will the a top-down shooter. I if you are completly new to everything about this I suggest you start with C# here and move on accordingly.

Using this site you gain the knowledge you need to make 2D games in Unity. We provide you with tutorials that were made by us but also provide links to other sources that you will find extremly helpful. In the video turotials is where you can find the links to various playlist that will help you on your journy.
